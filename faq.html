What happens to my data if I cancel?

If you cancel our services, any data that we have collected or stored on your behalf will be handled according to our data retention policy. We take data privacy and security seriously, and we will ensure that your data is handled in accordance with applicable laws and regulations. Before canceling our services, we will work with you to discuss the data transfer or deletion process. Depending on the type of data and your preferences, we can assist you in transferring ownership of the data, exporting it to your own systems, or securely deleting it from our servers. Rest assured, we will take all necessary steps to protect your data and uphold your privacy rights throughout the cancellation process. If you have any specific concerns or questions about data retention or deletion, please don't hesitate to reach out to us. We're here to help.

We provide regular reporting and analysis to track progress and make data-driven decisions to optimize performance.
